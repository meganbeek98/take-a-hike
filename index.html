Where to go?

"Why should I, 'Take a Hike', in Ohio?"

In addition to a Great Lakes coastline, rivers, and forests; hiking trails in Ohio include cool caves, spectacular waterfalls, deeply carved gorges, and grasslands dotted with wildflowers.
These picturesque settings are nearly perfect for hikes with friends and family, or just exploring on one's own. Get out there and experience how mother nature has graced The Buckeye State!

Ohio's Regional Parks ('Metroparks'): Ease of Access = Less Physical Demand.

In the United States, a regional park is sometimes referred to as a "metropolitan park" (metropark), or as an "open space reserve". Ohio's metroparks are public agencies serving the citizens of Ohio by providing a regional system of clean, safe, natural area parks. The mission of Metro Parks is to conserve open spaces, while providing places and opportunities that encourage people to discover and experience nature.
