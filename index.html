Where to go?

From the beautiful shores and islands of Lake Erie, through the rolling hills of the Hocking Valley, all the way to the waters of the great Ohio River, the Buckeye State is a magical place. Nationally, interest and participation in outdoor activities, especially camping, hiking, and kayaking, is on the rise. Each year, hundreds of thousands of visitors enjoy a variety of outdoor activities such as camping, hiking, biking, swimming, boating and much, much more at Ohio State Parks.
